Projects

Effect of News & Social Media on movement of stocks | Research Project | Praxis Business School | Jan 2019 - Current

Learning under the guidance of Dr. Sayantani Roy, the goal of this ongoing research project is to quantify if and how much do the stocks move when they appear in the news and social media. Valence(Intensity) based sentiment analysis is performed on stock news found in newspapers, google search results and social media (twitter)nand regression is performed to see how the stocks move with news.
Tech Stack: Python, VADER (Valence Aware Dictionary for Sentiment Reasoning), Numpy, Pandas, Matplotlib, Tensorflow, Beautiful Soup, Twitter Scraper and other basic python packages.

Text Summarisation using Opinion Mining for Business Intelligence | May 2017

To reduce the time taken by Business Analysts in reading and summarising hundreds of consumer reviews comments and tweets, this project intelligently summarises the data, topic-wise based on various NLP, topic modelling and sentiment analysis methods. The easy UI, takes a dump of data as input or a website link and parses the content in it. The algorithm performs topic modelling and then summaries the data based on those topics.
Tech Stack: Technology and Packages: Java, MALLET, Classifier4J, JSoup
Algorithms: LDA and TF-IDF

Autonomous Stock Brokerage | July 2017 | JP Morgan Chase & Co

Done under the guidance of University Outreach programme by JP Morgan Chase & Co, the goal of the project was to reduce the human involvement in trading by automatically trading stocks : buy and sell stocks based on various intelligent machine learning solutions which helped to maximise profits, reduce trading costs and achieve consistency. After Cross validation was performed to pick the best algorithm on 10 year stock data, KNN and Random Forest which gave maximum accuracies were picked. After backtesting, the algorithm predicted and performed buy and sell of stocks.
Tech Stack Technology and Packages: Python, Sci-kit learn, Pandas, Numpy, matplotlib
Algorithms Random Forest, QDA, SVM, KNN, Logistic Regression

Program Evaluation: Do Binge Watchers order food on an online app? | December 2018

Prediction if an online desert app should sponsor a trending TV Series. Project assesses if binge watchers order food online and if there are any other factors which influence viewers to watch the sponsor's ad and order food.
